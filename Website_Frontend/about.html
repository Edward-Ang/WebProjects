About us

Crispy Restaurant

We are team Frontenders that came from humble beginnings. Equipped with nothing but sheer perseverance, we took a leap of faith by quitting our full time job to start a stall to sell burgers back in 2020. Crispy Burger commenced operations in Malaysia in 2020 with the operating of the first restaurant at Ipoh, Perak. Today, our team, Frontenders operates more than 50 Crispy restaurant in Malaysia where customers across the country can enjoy our Crispy Burger. Our restaurant expanded into 3 countries includes Singapore, Thailand and Indonesia. You can choose your burger by filling meats, cheese, vegetables and any condiments. Our unique is we can let the customers to choose their toppings randomly into their burger.
